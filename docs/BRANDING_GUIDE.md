## üçΩÔ∏è Meal Presentation Guidelines

### For AI Image Generation

When generating professional meal images, maintain these elements:

#### Container & Setup
```
- Black rectangular meal prep container
- Clear transparent lid (optional in shot)
- Professional white label with Liberty Meal Prep branding
- Shot from 45-degree angle or top-down view
- Natural daylight lighting
- Clean, neutral background (white or light gray)
```

#### Food Styling
```
- Realistic portions (not oversized)
- Clear separation of protein, carbs, and vegetables
- Vibrant, fresh-looking ingredients
- Slight steam effect for hot meals (optional)
- Garnishes that match the meal description
- No artificial shine or unrealistic presentation
```

#### Photography Quality
```
- High resolution (1024x1024 minimum)
- Sharp focus on food
- Natural color saturation
- Professional food photography lighting
- Shallow depth of field (food in focus, background slightly blurred)
```
